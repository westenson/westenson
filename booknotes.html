book notes

i've found the only true way to learn is by reflecting.
this page was inspired by derek sivers.
i highly recommend:


my goal is to have in-depth notes for each of the following and all futures books i read. this will take time.

East of Eden

In progress...

Why Nations Fail

In progress...

When Breath Becomes Air

A heartbreaking story of the end of a man's life.

Fund Spy

Good overview of mutual fund investing by one of the most respected companies in the business, Morningstar .

Innovators

History of the computer and the internet. Must read.

The Ragged Edge of the Night

Historical fiction of resistance in Nazi Germany. I found it anticlimactic.

Outliers

An incredible book for understanding success in the modern world. Must read for parents.

The Intelligent Investor

Understanding the stock market and investing over the long run. The book that many of the greatest investors started with.

All the Light We Cannot See

WWII historical fiction. A touching story, although I didn't find it as enthralling as most reviewers did.

Origin Story

A brief history of the universe starting with the big bang, told wonderfully by David Christian.

Beneath a Scarlet Sky

An unbelievable story of a German boy involed in the resistance against the Nazis. Nearly read in one sitting, I couldn't put it down.

Principals

A must-read for business owners and for those interested in how Ray Dalio thinks.

Barbarians at the Gate

Good for understanding leveraged buyouts through the story of one of the largest in history.

The Hillbilly Elegy

Broadened perspective on the lives of those less fortunate. Similar lessons to The Glass Castle. Incredible page-turner.

10% Happier

Compelling story about how meditation and mindfulness can be life-changing.

Elon Musk Biography

Love him or hate him, Elon has changed the world. Unsurprisingly, this book is already outdated. Nevertheless, he's an inspiration and we need to clone him.

The Big Short

Everyone should understand the Great Recession. Michael Lewis does an incredibly entertaining job of doing so.

One Page Financial Plan

If you're new to investing or haven't started, this is a quick read with the basics.

Man's Search for Meaning

Incredible true story about a Jewish man's life in a concentration camp. We are so lucky.

12 Rules of Life

Jordan Peterson is incredibly interesting and offers good tips for a fulfilling life. Although, I found his opinions too strong and verbose.

Being Mortal

Excellent discussion on the state of healthcare in the U.S. and problems with how we treat our elderly. Atul Gawanda is a true role model.

The Glass Castle

Broadened perspective on the lives of those less fortunate. Incredible read that I could not put down.

Leonardo DaVinci

Biography of one of the most incredible men in known history. Those who can find intersections of thought have the greatest chance of success.

Life 3.0

Highly hypothetical story about the future of A.I. Entertaining, scary, and insightful.

Steve Jobs Biography

The story of Steve Jobs, one of the world's greatest visionaries.

Evicted

America's issues with housing and poverty, set in Milwaukee, WI.

How to Win Friends and Influence People

Great life advice on wielding influence over others with a simple shift of perspective.


sorted in chronological order, from most recently read to least recently read
